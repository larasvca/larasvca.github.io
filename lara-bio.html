Hello! I’m Lara Svensson Calzada, and I’m currently working on academic projects involving humanoid robots and programming them to perform helper tasks. My interests lie in teaching robots to execute tasks once objects are identified, focusing on how these robots can assist people in practical ways.

In addition, I’m exploring Turing machines and developing custom syntax simulators. My academic journey also includes building a professional web presence, with current projects involving GitHub Pages as a platform for hosting and showcasing my work.

I’m passionate about the intersection of technology and human interaction, and I’m always seeking opportunities to push the boundaries of robotics and computer science.
